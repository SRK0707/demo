WALL PANELS

Acoustic CNS Panels

Acoustic CNS cut wall panels are precision-cut panels designed to improve sound absorption while adding aesthetic appeal to interior spaces. These panels are typically crafted from high-density materials such as MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard), plywood, or fiber cement boards and feature CNC (Computer Numerical Control) cutting technology to create intricate patterns, perforations, or grooves that enhance their acoustic performance.

Acoustic Screens

Acoustic Screens are soundproof barriers or panels designed to absorb, block, or redirect sound to reduce noise levels and enhance the acoustics of a space. They are often used in environments where noise control is crucial, such as offices, studios, factories, hospitals, or public areas. Acoustic screens come in various shapes, sizes, and materials, offering flexibility in how they can be deployed to manage sound effectively.

Printed Acoustic Panels

Printed Acoustic Panels are a visually appealing and effective solution for controlling sound in a variety of spaces. These panels combine the benefits of sound absorption with custom graphics or artwork, making them a popular choice in environments where both acoustics and aesthetics matter.

CEILINGS

Hanging Clouds

Hanging clouds, also known as acoustic cloud panels, are an innovative solution for controlling sound in large spaces, particularly those with high ceilings or open designs. These panels are designed to reduce noise, control reverberation, and improve the overall acoustics in a room, making them an ideal choice for venues like concert halls, offices, classrooms, theaters, and more.

Acoustic Cell ceilings

An acoustic cell ceiling is a type of suspended ceiling system designed to improve the sound quality and control noise levels within a space. These ceilings typically consist of individual panels or tiles arranged in a grid system that are engineered to absorb sound and reduce reverberation. Acoustic cell ceilings are commonly used in environments where sound control is critical, such as in offices, schools, hospitals, concert halls, and other public spaces.

Acoustic baffle

An acoustic baffle is a sound-absorbing or sound-diffusing structure used to control sound in various environments. These devices are commonly found in spaces like concert halls, studios, offices, and industrial areas to manage noise and improve acoustics. They are designed to reduce noise levels, control reverberation, and create a more controlled sound environment.
